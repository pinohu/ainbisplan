## Integrity boundary

The platform deliberately does not mark business-specific research, market validation, primary-source verification, expert credentials, legal/clinical/financial approval, backlinks, customer proof, external service configuration, or production deployments as complete unless their actual evidence exists. Those activities require real external facts, accountable people, credentials, accounts, or production systems.

Automated implementation therefore advances plans to governed execution and creates enforceable queues, but it never fabricates the evidence needed to pass authority or indexability gates.
